About Us

ACACIA PREMIER HOTEL.
Acacia Premier Hotel is a cozy Kenyan restaurant located in the heart of downtown.
Our menu features authentic Italian dishes made with fresh, high-quality ingredients, and our cozy and intimate atmosphere makes for the perfect setting for a romantic dinner or a night out with friends..

  • Menu Highlights

    Our menu features a variety of classic Italian dishes, including handmade pasta dishes like our popular fettuccine alfredo and lasagna alla Bolognese. We also offer a selection of wood-fired pizzas, fresh salads, and delicious seafood dishes like our grilled salmon with lemon and capers.

  • Customer Reviews

    "Acacia Premier Hotel is hands down the best Italian restaurant in town. The food is always fresh and delicious, and the staff is incredibly friendly and attentive. I highly recommend the gnocchi with pesto sauce!"
    "Acacia Premier Hotel for the first time last night and was blown away by the quality of the food and the welcoming atmosphere. The homemade pasta was out of this world, and the tiramisu was the perfect end to a perfect meal."

  • Pricing

    Our prices are reasonable and range from $15 to $30 per entree, with appetizers and desserts priced between $5 and $12.

  • Hours and Location

    Accacia Premier Hotel is open Monday through Saturday from 5:00 pm to 10:00 pm and is located at 123 Main Street in downtown.
